Mission


It is the mission of St. George Early Learning is to nurture the “whole child” by providing a safe, affordable and developmentally appropriate environment for all families in the community. Our focus is to provide a stimulating, educational experience that fosters and supports each child’s unique social/emotional, physical and cognitive development. Our goal is to support and foster children’s desire to learn which leads to them becoming life-long learners. In doing so we support families in their efforts to help their children reach their goals and beyond. Our staff is committed to the families we serve, providing support and encouragement. Striving each day to provide an environment where parents feel secure leaving their children by encouraging parent involvement.
